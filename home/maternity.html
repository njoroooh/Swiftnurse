What We Offer

Comprehensive Maternity Services

From the first trimester to postpartum healing, our certified nurses provide full-spectrum maternal healthcare at home.

🤰

Prenatal Care

Professional monitoring and support throughout pregnancy for both mother and baby.

  • Weekly vital signs monitoring (BP, pulse, weight)
  • Fetal heartbeat monitoring at home
  • Nutritional counseling & meal planning
  • Antenatal exercises & breathing techniques
  • Edema assessment & management
  • Lab specimen collection & coordination
  • High-risk pregnancy monitoring
👶

Labour & Delivery Support

Skilled birth assistance and comfort care during your labour journey.

  • Early labour assessment & monitoring
  • Contraction timing & pain management
  • Accompany & support at delivery facility
  • Comfort techniques — massage, positioning
  • Emotional & psychological support
  • Birth plan coordination with hospital team
  • Post-delivery immediate newborn care
🌸

Postpartum Care

Expert recovery support and newborn care in the critical weeks after birth.

  • Postpartum wound & episiotomy care
  • C-section incision dressing & monitoring
  • Breastfeeding consultation & support
  • Newborn bathing, cord, & skin care
  • Postpartum depression screening
  • Uterine involution monitoring
  • Baby feeding schedule & weight tracking
💊

Medication & Health Management

Safe administration and monitoring of maternity medications at home.

  • Iron, folic acid & supplement administration
  • Gestational diabetes insulin management
  • Blood pressure medication monitoring
  • IV fluid administration if prescribed
  • Medication side-effect assessment
  • Coordination with OB/GYN physician
🧠

Mental & Emotional Wellness

Holistic emotional support for the mental health of new mothers.

  • Postpartum depression & anxiety screening
  • Counseling & emotional support sessions
  • Stress management techniques
  • Family bonding & attachment guidance
  • Referral to mental health professionals
  • Support group recommendations
📋

Education & Training

Empowering mothers and families with the knowledge they need.

  • Newborn care training for parents
  • Safe sleep practices & SIDS prevention
  • Breastfeeding & formula preparation
  • Immunisation schedule guidance
  • Danger signs recognition training
  • Postpartum self-care education
Your Care Journey

A Roadmap Through Every Stage

SwftNurse supports you from the very first weeks of pregnancy all the way through postpartum recovery.

Weeks 1 – 12

First Trimester Monitoring

Initial health assessment, booking visit, blood pressure and weight baseline, nutritional counseling, morning sickness management, and lifestyle guidance.

🌱
💛
Weeks 13 – 26

Second Trimester Support

Fetal heartbeat monitoring, gestational diabetes screening support, anaemia management, antenatal exercises, and birth plan discussions begin.

Weeks 27 – 40

Third Trimester Preparation

Intensive monitoring of blood pressure, fetal movement counts, oedema assessment, labour preparation classes, hospital bag guidance, and birth plan finalisation.

🌟
❤️
Labour Day

Labour & Delivery Support

Active labour monitoring, pain management techniques, emotional support, hospital escort and coordination with the delivery team for a safe birth.

Days 1 – 7

Immediate Postpartum

Daily home visits, wound care, breastfeeding initiation support, newborn assessment, jaundice monitoring, and postpartum complication screening.

🤱
🌸
Weeks 2 – 6

Extended Postpartum Recovery

Continued wound healing, family adjustment support, postpartum depression monitoring, baby development milestones tracking, and discharge planning.

Clinical Procedures

Evidence-Based Procedures We Perform

All procedures are performed by registered nurses following internationally recognised maternal healthcare standards.

🩺
01

Vital Signs Monitoring

Regular blood pressure, pulse, temperature, and respiratory rate checks for both mother and newborn.

💉
02

Blood Specimen Collection

Home phlebotomy for full blood count, blood glucose, HIV, hepatitis, and other prenatal panels.

🎧
03

Fetal Heart Rate Assessment

Doppler monitoring of fetal heartbeat to assess baby's wellbeing throughout pregnancy.

🩹
04

Wound & Episiotomy Care

Sterile dressing, cleaning, and monitoring of perineal tears, episiotomies, and caesarean incisions.

🍼
05

Infant Feeding Support

Latch assessment, nipple care, milk supply guidance, and formula preparation education.

🛁
06

Newborn Hygiene Care

Sponge and tub bathing, umbilical cord stump care, nappy care, and skin condition assessment.

📊
07

Growth & Development Tracking

Regular weight checks, length measurements, head circumference, and developmental milestone screening.

🧪
08

Urine & Blood Glucose Testing

Point-of-care urinalysis and blood glucose monitoring for gestational diabetes management.

💊
09

Medication Administration

Safe oral, IM, and IV medication administration as prescribed by the attending obstetrician.

🧠
10

Psychological Assessment

Edinburgh Postnatal Depression Scale (EPDS) screening and mental health referral coordination.

📝
11

Comprehensive Documentation

Detailed clinical notes, care plans, and real-time reporting shared with the mother's healthcare team.

🚨
12

Emergency Triage & Referral

Rapid assessment of danger signs (eclampsia, PPH, sepsis) and immediate emergency coordination.
