    Daily Tips for Chronic Condition Management

    💧

    Stay Hydrated

    Adequate water intake supports kidney function and blood pressure control.

    🚶

    Move Daily

    Even 15 minutes of gentle walking improves cardiovascular health.

    Never Skip Medication

    Consistent medication adherence prevents complications.

    😴

    Prioritise Sleep

    Quality sleep regulates blood pressure and blood sugar.

    Chronic Care FAQs

    What makes chronic condition management different?+
    Chronic condition management requires specialist clinical knowledge, ongoing monitoring and proactive intervention — not just personal care assistance.
    Can SwiftNurse manage multiple conditions?+
    Yes, absolutely. SwiftNurse creates integrated care plans that address all conditions holistically.
    How does SwiftNurse work with my GP?+
    SwiftNurse acts as an extension of your medical team, sharing regular clinical reports.
    Is there a minimum commitment period?+
    We recommend 4 weeks but impose no fixed long-term contracts.
